PowerUp - iOS

PowerUp is an educational mobile game app that seeks to empower young girls to take charge of their reproductive health and self-esteem by navigating the life of their Avatar.

Currently, this documentation is only for the features added during GSoC 2018.

Moving forward, the powerup-ios could be updated such that the entire code base could also be documented and featured here.
